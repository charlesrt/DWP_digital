Commercial

The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) impacts upon the lives of virtually every resident in the UK, every day, helping people to find employment, deliver their welfare support (e.g. Universal Credit) and administer state run pensions, presenting us with the unique opportunity to make strategic changes in how we deliver services across the country. We employ over 84,000 staff across the UK, and our total spend across these services in 2014/15 was £175bn. As part of this organisation, the Commercial Directorate (CD) plays a critical role in shaping how services are delivered across a variety of contractual arrangements (annual procurement spend of circa £3bn on 3rd party suppliers).
