Senior Civil Service Come and lead the future of the Civil Service

The Civil Service plays a crucial role in supporting the wellbeing, security and prosperity of the country.

The Senior Civil Service is looking for people who have ambition, motivation and passion. You will be leading some of Europe’s largest projects, changing the way the public interacts with government services and transforming the way we use data and technology to build a Civil Service for the future.

The Senior Civil Service provides high-level support to government ministers, assists in the development and implementation of policy and delivers safe and secure public services to our citizens.

This is an opportunity to join government at an incredibly interesting time. It’s one of the most challenging and rewarding places to work in the UK today. We need a unique combination of leadership and hands-on technical expertise to lead our digital transformation.

We employ 3,900 professionals at this level, in 55 different government departments and agencies
